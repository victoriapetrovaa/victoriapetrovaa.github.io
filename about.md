# About

Hi, I am Victoria! I am trained as a cognitive scientist to unite psychology and computer and information science. I am now using this expertise to engage with artificial intelligence topics. I am most passionate about research topics in the psychology of large-scale AI risks.
My priority now is helping AGI strategy initiatives become more persuasive to the general public. I analyze both the cognitive biases (ie. motivated reasoning) and the emotional blockage preventing most people from engaging with AI risks on an intellectual level. I have written about these topics on LessWrong. 

I am currently co-authoring an article with Seth Herd, neuroscientist and AI safety researcher at Astera Institute.

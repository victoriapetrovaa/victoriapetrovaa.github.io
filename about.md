# About

Hi, I am Victoria! I am trained as a cognitive scientist, and I am now using my interdisciplinary background to engage with AI safety topics. I am most passionate about research topics in the psychology of large-scale AI risks.
My priority is helping AGI strategy initiatives become more persuasive to the general public. I believe public pressure will bring about the speediest form of AI governance. 
I analyze both the cognitive biases (ie. motivated reasoning) and the emotional blockage preventing most people from engaging with AI risks on an intellectual level. I have written about these topics on LessWrong. 

I am currently co-authoring an article with Seth Herd, neuroscientist and AI safety researcher at Astera Institute.
